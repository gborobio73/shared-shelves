FAQ

Why this app?

I think books are to be read. As I have a lot of them, someone else might want to read those same books as well. Books are expensive here in Finland and the selection of, for example, Spanish books is small. I also think this is a nice way to meet other people.

How does this work?

You can add your books to the bookshelf by clicking "Add book". Insert the ISBN (with or without hyphens) and the book information will be imported. Sometimes the information cannot be found but I am constantly working to improve the search.

What if I am interested in a book?

Just click the button "Let the owner know!" and an email will be sent to both you and the owner. You can take it from there.

What do I do once I have sold a book?

Please remove it from the bookshelf, as it is not available anymore.

What do you do with my Google account?

It provides a secure way to identify you and to send you emails. Nothing else.

Is this app for free?

Yep.
